The Big Day - Saturday, July 12, 2014

Ceremony - 5:30 PM
McMichael Canadian Art Gallery
10365 Islington Ave
Kleinburg, Vaughan, Ontario

Please join us on Saturday, July 12th at the McMichael Art Gallery in Kleinburg, Ontario where we will hit one out of the park when we say our I do's.

The McMichael Art Gallery is located just minutes west of Canada's Wonderland, near Islington Ave. and Major MacKenzie Dr.

Click here to view a map of the area.
 

Reception - 7:00 PM

Following the ceremony will be a cocktail hour where drinks and hors d'oeuvres will be served outside the main reception area. During this time, there will be a gallery of the Group of Seven available for viewing for anyone interested (no drinks/food permitted in the gallery area).

After the cocktail hour, please join us in the main hall to begin the reception where there will be an open bar and wine on the tables during dinner.
